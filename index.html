      How to Play Just One

      Objective

      Cooperative party game where players work together to help one player guess mystery words using one-word clues.

      How to Play

      1. Word Holder: One player clicks "Get Word" to see the mystery word
      2. Clue Givers: Other players click "Type Clue" and enter one-word clues
      3. Review: All clues appear in a list. Identical/invalid clues are highlighted red and must be rejected (not shown to the word holder)
      4. Show Clue: Click "Show Clue" to display your clue to the word holder
      5. Guess: Word holder makes ONE guess based on visible clues
      6. Score: Click "End Round" and select: ✓ Correct (+1), → Pass (0), ✗ Fail (-1) based on whether they guessed right, passed, or guessed wrong

      Invalid Clues (Cancelled)

      • Identical clues (including word families, plurals, gender variants)
      • The mystery word in different spelling or language
      • Words from the same family as the mystery word
      • Invented words or phonetically identical words

      App Features

      • Online: Share room code to play with friends
      • Offline: Pass device around for local play
      • Hold to Blur: Temporarily hide word on word screen
